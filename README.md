# Advertising-Portfolio-Project_Analysing-Google-Ads-Sales-Data
Analysing Synthetic Google Ads Raw Sales Records in 2024 (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/nayakganesh007/google-ads-sales-dataset).

_In this Portfolio Project I would like to demonstrate my authentic analytical process and how I derive actionable insights from this dataset_.

After a rigorous analytical review of our recent advertising and sales data, I have processed and audited the raw dataset (resolving significant naming inconsistencies and data formatting errors) to provide a clear view of our current financial standing and growth potential.
1. Executive KPI Dashboard (Consolidated)
Our core performance metrics indicate a highly efficient and healthy advertising ecosystem:
•	Total Ad Investment: $493,967.37
•	Gross Revenue Generated: $3,446,142.00
•	Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): 6.98x (For every $1 spent, we generate nearly $7 in revenue).
•	Average Cost Per Acquisition (CPA): $32.95 * Overall Conversion Rate: 4.92%
2. Strategic Insights by Segment
A. Device Performance: The Cross-Platform Advantage
One of the most striking findings is the stability of our performance across all user platforms. While many companies see a "Mobile-only" or "Desktop-only" success, our revenue is evenly distributed:
•	Desktop: Leads with a 7.00x ROAS and the lowest CPA ($32.67). This remains our highest-intent platform.
•	Tablet & Mobile: Following closely at 6.98x and 6.94x respectively.
•	Analyst Note: This suggests our landing pages and checkout processes are excellently optimized for all screen sizes, providing a resilient foundation for scaling.
B. Keyword Efficiency: Revenue Drivers
The keyword analysis reveals a strong focus on high-intent educational queries:
1.	"data analytics course": Highest ROI keyword (7.12x ROAS).
2.	"learn data analytics": Highest total revenue driver ($608k).
3.	"data anaytics training": Exceptional efficiency with over 2,400 conversions.
3. Data Integrity & Analytical Rigor
The raw data contained significant noise, including four variations of campaign names and multiple geographical misspellings (e.g., "Hydrebad"). By consolidating these into a single "Data Analytics Course" campaign, we discovered that the performance is much more centralized and stable than fragmented reporting would suggest.
4. Strategic Recommendations for Long-term Sustainability
Based on the 6.98x ROAS, I propose the following three-pillar strategy for the upcoming fiscal year:
1.	Aggressive Scaling of Desktop Budgets: Since Desktop provides the lowest CPA and highest ROAS, I recommend a 15% budget reallocation toward Desktop during peak conversion hours to maximize net profit.
2.	Keyword Expansion: We should launch "Exact Match" campaigns for our top 3 keywords ("learn data analytics", "data analytics course", and "data analytics training") to lower costs further by increasing our Quality Score.
3.	Sustainability via CPA Caps: To ensure long-term profitability, we should implement an automated "CPA Cap" of $35.00. Currently, our average is $32.95, giving us a safety margin to scale while maintaining a minimum 6x ROAS.
